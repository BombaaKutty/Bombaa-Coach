About Us

Bombaa Coach is Kenya’s largest transporter in the country by bringing ease and convenience to millions of Kenyan travellers. We have the most comfortable buses on the Kenyan road with more than enough legroom to ensure that you reach your destination fully relaxed. Our amazing line of luxury models buses with free WiFi, Electrical Charging Outlets, DVD players, reclining seats, air conditioned and flat screen TV’s. Our mission is to provide excellent, safe, and reliable transportation to all our clients. Thank you for visiting our Website.
